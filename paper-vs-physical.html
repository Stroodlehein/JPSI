What This Measures
COMEX — Paper Market
COMEX silver futures are contracts for future delivery, mostly settled in cash. The price is the global benchmark — but it represents paper, not physical metal in your hand.
MSPI-B — Street Market
JPMI's MSPI-B tracks real peer-to-peer sold prices for 1oz .999 silver bullion on Mercari Japan. These are actual transactions — what real buyers in Japan are paying today.
Why The Premium Exists
Physical silver costs more than COMEX because of fabrication costs, dealer margins, and real supply and demand. When the premium expands, physical silver is tight. Japan's premium is uniquely visible here.
