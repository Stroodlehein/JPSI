What This Measures
COMEX — The Paper Market
COMEX silver futures represent contracts for future silver delivery. Most are settled in cash and never result in physical silver changing hands. The COMEX price is the globally-referenced benchmark for silver.
MSPI-B — The Physical Market
The JPMI MSPI-B tracks real peer-to-peer sold prices for 1oz .999 silver bullion coins on Mercari Japan. These are actual transactions — real buyers paying real money for physical silver in Japan today.
Why The Premium Exists
Physical silver trades at a premium to COMEX because of fabrication costs, dealer margins, supply chain friction, and real-world supply and demand dynamics. When premiums expand, it signals that physical silver is tight relative to paper market pricing. The Japan Physical Premium captures this signal directly from retail market transactions.
