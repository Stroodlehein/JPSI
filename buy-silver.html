Interpretation

How should these options be interpreted?

Where pricing efficiency is the primary objective, BullionVault provides the most direct route. Where secure allocated ownership is the objective, GoldBroker fits more naturally. Where coins or bars must be shipped directly, APMEX represents the retail-delivery route.

Why does the page lead with silver when these platforms also support gold?

Because JPMI’s edge begins with Japan’s physical silver premium. That premium is the market signal creating the point of entry. Once inside the decision set, broader precious-metals exposure remains possible, including gold and other metals.
